Policy

Internal-only line

The public surface can explain that gated internal material exists. It should not republish internal benchmark data or present it as an academic dataset.

Strict boundary: R012-E benchmark data is internal self-evolution reference only. Public pages should route readers to the gated vault and keep public claims grounded in open pages, ledgers, or raw run artifacts. Do not publish raw prompts, operator paths, private endpoints, credentials, or unredacted internal run state.
